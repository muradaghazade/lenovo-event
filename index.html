Business Innovation Day by ThinkPad - 11 June

Business Innovation Bootcamp by ThinkPad

(11 June - 11 July) Register

About

Think Pad

The Business Innovation Bootcamp by ThinkPad will be co-organized by Lenovo and Microsoft.

The Bootcamp will provide training on business, innovation, and startups to people with business ideas, as well as the necessary knowledge and skills to help them build their own businesses. In addition, Lenovo will present laptops, tablets, and other gifts as the primary technical equipment to put their knowledge into practice.

Participants will be intensively trained by Tariyel Aghazada, an expert in Innovative business and startups, throughout the course of the month-long program and will be able to bring their ideas to the project.

The Representative of Microsoft in Azerbaijan and a leading specialist in the Innovation Sector will be a guest at the Opening Ceremony of the program.

Registration starts (May 16), Registration ends (June 7), Selection Process (June 8-10), Event Day ( June 11), Program starts ( June 13), Program ends ( July 11)
